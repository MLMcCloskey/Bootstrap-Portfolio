About

I'm a recent graduate from Georgia Tech trained in the MERN stack which includes Mongo.db, Express, React.js, and Node.js to develop websites and applications. I am also adept with MySQL, jQuery, Handlebars, Mocha and Chai unit testing, Axios, and Cheerio.js.

If you are interested in hiring a web developer for a project you have in mind, feel free to reach out to me. You can send me an e-mail at the 'Contact' page of this site, or reach me on social media by clicking any of the links below.
